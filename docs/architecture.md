**Product name:** Telegram  
**Link to the product's website:** web.telegram.org  
**Short description of the product:**  
Telegram is a cloud-based, cross-platform instant messaging service focused on speed, security, and high-capacity communication, featuring end-to-end encrypted secret chats, large group chats (up to 200,000 members), and broadcast channels.
